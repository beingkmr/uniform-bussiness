Uniform Manufacturer & Supplier

Your Trusted Partner From Design to Delivery

Headquartered in Bengaluru with roots in Kolar Gold Fields, Planet10 Uniforms is a full-service uniform manufacturer serving institutions across Karnataka, India, and export-ready programs. We combine bespoke design, precision embroidery, and high-fidelity printing with production workflows built for efficiency and repeatability.

From individual reorders to multi-campus contracts, our team manages sampling, size charts, bulk cutting, stitching, finishing, and dispatch — with transparent communication at every stage. Competitive pricing, consistent quality, and responsive client service define how we work with every partner.

  • Quality assurance at fabric, stitch, and branding stages
  • Customized solutions — logos, colors, and garment sets
  • Timely delivery with production tracking
  • Competitive pricing for bulk and annual contracts
  • Dedicated support from inquiry to final dispatch
